The Problem: Hate Against Asian Individuals
During the coronavirus outbreak that became a global pandemic on March 11, 2020, attacks against Asian Americans have heightened. Data compiled from 15 cities throughout the United States indicates that reported hate crimes increased by 169% in just the first quarter of 2021. Numerous individuals have had this pain inflicted upon them by these assaults, and countless others live in fear that they or their loved ones may become victims.
